Do I need to be great at Math to learn coding?

No. While understanding concepts of variables in Alegbra are nice, it is not required for you to learn how to program. Most students that take an active part in their education learn on-the-fly.

Is there a lot of homework?

Yes and No. The class is project driven; therefore, you are responsible for being self-directed and learning how to perform research to discover answers to problems. However, traditional homework assignments are not given out and turned in.

How important is this class?

Imagine if we still lived as farmers and nobody taught farming in school. Now consider that we live in a technology driven world. It is important for you to learn new tools and skills so you can be workforce competitive and economically viable.

Will I learn how to hack?

Part of living in a digital world is understanding how attacks take place and the ethical ramifications of cyber security. In short, ask yourself why would you want to hack others? If your answer is malicious, then you will not be taught hacking skills.

What about STEM honors?

Select seniors will be able to complete a STEM senior project. The requirements are 4 years of Science, 4 years of Math, and at least a 3.0 cummulative GPA. If you are interested, speak with Mr. Kam no later than the first week of school to discuss your options. Remember, not all students will qualify even though you are a senior.
